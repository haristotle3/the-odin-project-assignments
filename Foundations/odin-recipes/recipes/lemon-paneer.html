Lemon Paneer Recipe

Description

Lemon paneer is a simple paneer dish, can be had as such for a filling evening snack. Very simple and easy recipe with paneer.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 cup Paneer
  • 2 teaspoon Garlic
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 3 tablespoon Capsicum
  • 3 Green chilli
  • 2 All purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon Corn flour
  • 1 teaspoon Lemon zest
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Sugar
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Turmeric
  • Cooking soda
  • 2 tablespoon Coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoon Spring onion
  • Oil as needed
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Keep paneer immersed in hot water until use. Meanwhile chop onion, capsicum, coriander leaves, spring onion. Slice green chilli diagonally.
  2. Further more, make a batter out of maida (all purpose flour), 1 tablespoon corn flour (corn starch), salt, 1/8 teaspoon turmeric, cooking soda, lemon zest, with water.
  3. Then dip each cube in the batter and deep fry in hot oil. You can fry few per batch. Drain over paper towel once crisp as well as golden in color.. Repeat to finish.
  4. In a kadai/ pan heat 1 tablespoon oil firstly. Then add garlic, green chillies. Fry till fragrant and add cubed onion, capsicum. Until onion turns translucent.
  5. Add 1 cup water, bring to boil. Then add fried paneer cubes.
  6. Dissolve 1 tablespoon corn flour in 2 tablespoon water. Stir in the boiling water.
  7. Once thick, garnish with chopped coriander, spring onion. Switch off the flame, squeeze the lemon juice.
